About Japan Combines

The Japan Combines is Japan's premiere sport testing and evaluation agency. We were created by Darrell Harris (link to Bio page), founder of OSMOSYS JAPAN. The JC were inspired by the NBA and NFL Draft Combines and it is our dream to offer premium testing services, goods and support to elite and everyday athletes in Japan and Asia. We have gathered a global team of top trainers and coaches that skill assessments events for athletes and coaches of various sports (Basketball, Soccer, Football, Lacrosse, Golf and more). JC is powered by Sports Testing Inc, the leading company in sport-specific, state-of-the-art athlete assessment technologies and services.
